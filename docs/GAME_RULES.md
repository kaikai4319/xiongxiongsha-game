# Xiongxiongsha Game Rules

## Overview
Xiongxiongsha is a multiplayer card game combining elements of strategy, deduction, and role-playing.

## Core Mechanics

### Players and Roles
- **Emperor**: The main role that other players try to assassinate
- **Peasants**: Common players who have no specific goal
- **Rebels**: Work together to eliminate the Emperor
- **Spies**: Try to eliminate everyone while appearing harmless

### Game Flow
1. Players are dealt roles secretly
2. Each round, players take turns performing actions
3. Players can attack, defend, or use special abilities
4. When a player's health reaches 0, they are eliminated
5. Game ends when a win condition is met

### Win Conditions
- **Emperor wins**: All rebels and spies are eliminated
- **Rebels win**: The Emperor is eliminated
- **Spy wins**: Only they and the Emperor remain
- **Peasants win**: The Emperor is defeated by others

### Health System
- Each player starts with 4 health
- Basic attacks deal 1 damage
- Special abilities can deal 2-3 damage
- Defense cards reduce incoming damage

### Card Types
- **Attack Cards**: Deal damage to other players
- **Defense Cards**: Reduce incoming damage
- **Ability Cards**: Special powers based on role
- **Utility Cards**: Support and helper cards

## Turn Structure
1. Draw Phase: Draw a card
2. Action Phase: Play cards or use abilities
3. Attack Phase: Deal damage if applicable
4. End Phase: Discard down to hand limit

## Special Rules
- Players eliminated in their turn still get to complete their turn
- Damage is applied instantly
- Cards are discarded after use unless otherwise noted
- Maximum hand size is 10 cards

## Strategy Tips
- Identify roles based on player behavior
- Balance offense and defense
- Use special abilities wisely
- Form temporary alliances when beneficial
